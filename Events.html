Major Events

Siquijor Day

This event celebrates the founding anniversary of Siquijor as a province. It typically includes parades, cultural performances, beauty pageants, and various competitions.

Bugwas Festival

Bugwas Festival is a week-long celebration held in Larena, Siquijor. It showcases the town's cultural heritage through street dancing, music performances, sports events, and beauty pageants. The festival aims to promote unity, peace, and prosperity among the locals.

Fiesta Celebrations

Each town in Siquijor celebrates its own fiesta in honor of its patron saint. These fiestas feature religious processions, traditional dances, music, feasting, and other cultural activities. The dates vary depending on the town and the patron saint.

Lazi Town Fiesta

Lazi, a municipality in Siquijor, celebrates its fiesta in honor of St. Isidore the Farmer. The fiesta includes various events such as cultural presentations, sports competitions, and religious processions.

San Juan By The Bay

This annual event in San Juan town features a beach party, live music, food stalls, and various water activities. It usually takes place in June and attracts both locals and tourists.

Healing Festival

Siquijor is famous for its traditional healers known as "mananambals" or "mangkukulams." The Healing Festival, usually held in August, showcases traditional healing practices, herbal medicine, and spiritual rituals.
